WEB DESIGN / INTERACTION

REPORTER WEB REDESIGN


PROJECT BRIEF

Reporter.rit.edu is Rochester Institute of Technology's leading print publication. Although they have a strong print presence on campus, their web presence is lacking in several aspects. As a designer, I was faced with the task of refreshing the Reporter site and give it a more organized feel and cleaner aesthetic.

The goal of this project was to dive into the Reporter site, analyze its problems across the board, and conjure a methodical solution to those problems. This was achieved through intense research of UI elements, stakeholders, development of low-fidelity and high-fidelity wireframes, and multiple stylized iterations.
