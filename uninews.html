WEB DESIGN

RIT UNIVERSITY
NEWS REDESIGN


PROJECT BRIEF

University News is much like any other online university news publication. However, it's biggest problem is viewers. It isn't quite clear to why this is a problem, but one problem that quickly became apparent was that the website was lacking a clear hierarchy of content - a topic which is discussed quite often in the field of design.

The topic of this project was to redesign University News and reapply content in the most logical hierarchy. After analysis of the old sites design, many obvious changes became clear. I aimed to create a visually pleasing design which focuses all important aspects user in the forefront of the design, while toning back some less important sections.
