PROJECT BRIEF

In design, we are often faced with several ways to portray our ideas. Sometimes, we must think outside the two-dimensional space and image objects in their native three-dimensional form. Often designers will find that 3D is a more practical way not only to portray a certain feel in their design, but also allows for their imagination to break free from traditional ways of tackling a problem through design.

These are a collection of 3D renderings I created over one semester. All objects were modeled in Cinema 4D from scratch for practice excercises as part of a 3D class where we learned the basics of modeling, rendering, lighting, and basic 3D animation.
