UI/UX DESIGN CONCEPT

PHONTOGRAPH
iOS REDESIGN


PROJECT BRIEF

Phontograph is a photo manipulation application that allows users to add fonts quickly and easily to their photos while on the fly. Although the quality of their application was lacking, I saw the potential of this application to bridge the gap between the everyday user and mainstream, complicated graphics editors such as Photoshop and Illustrator.

I decided to re-design the application for a class project which required us to find an application that needed a refreshment from the iTunes app store.
