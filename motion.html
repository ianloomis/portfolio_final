MOTION GRAPHICS

2D/3D MOTION PROJECTS


PROJECT BRIEF

Ah, video! We all love videos! I have been fascinated by motion my enitre life, buying my first video camera when I was 12. Shooting video was nothing more than a hobby to me. It wasn't until I discovered the world of motion graphics that I would find myself opening up After Effects again. Now instead of keying out green backgrounds, I was keyframing text boxes and 3D objects. This medium allows for a certain expression that is not achievable by flat or 3D design and can convey all the emotions a film can.

These are a collection of motion graphics pieces I have created since beginning New Media at RIT.
