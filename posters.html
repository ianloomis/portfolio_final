POSTER DESIGNS


PROJECT BRIEF

Almost all of the work I face in a day to day basis is meant for the screen, but there isn't something quite like seeing your work on some wall somewhere. Every designer has faced the poster at some point, whether it's making that flyer for your moms quilt guild or advertising your next big thing. In a world where computers are quickly taking precednce over print, there is a sense of humility for the medium. However, the poster allows for a unique way to communicate with your audience that a computer can't always do.

These are a collection of posters I have designed since beginning school. Posters range in topics, but I feel I have developed a style that I can go to when it comes to tackling print mediums.
