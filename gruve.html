UI/UX DESIGN CONCEPT

GRUVE


PROJECT BRIEF

If there isn't one thing I love most, it's music. With hundreds of music streaming sources streaming every genre of music out there, picking a music application can be tricky. Sure you could go and listen to Top 100, but what if you want to hear what's going on with new artists. New artists are emerging every day through sources like Bandcamp and SoundCloud. I wanted an app that pooled the best of these underground sources.

Music is all about the groove, hence Gruve, the music application that pulls from new and emerging artists and gives the user the best of the underground sound. Gruve is not only a music player, but also a community. A place where listeners can be up to date with what new artists are doing, as well as a communication point for artists to connect with their fans.
